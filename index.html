Project Idea

AI SMART HOME

Overview

This project is going to be about a home having “AI”, the one of the best technologies of the coming decades. This type of home will be able to help anyone of any age having any disability. This home can be controlled by your phone, remote as well as your voice. This is going to be an anti-theft home which will ensure the protection of your family because nowadays it’s very important. This tech will make your life easier so that you can spend time with your family and focus on your studies and work rather than the stress of maintaining your house.

Motivation

So there are a lot of people in the world who need this technology in their life to make a big difference. The people who are in need can be those ones who love “AI” and aged people who can’t help themselves, people and children with a disabilities and families with a lot of people too. In 2020, there were an estimated 727 million persons aged 65 years or over worldwide. This number is projected to more than double by 2050, reaching over 1.5 billion persons. The share of older persons in the global population is expected to increase from 9.3 per cent in 2020 to 16.0 per cent in 2050. There are a total number of 15% people in the world who are disabled and this “AI SMART HOME” can give them a nice life. People with big houses often have to take leave from work to maintain their house and this keeps them busy. This shows the need for the technology. Everyone might think that this “AI SMART HOME” would be expensive but to make this project work for everyone, the government must take this project into action and help their people.

Description

This “AI SMART HOME” will be a home in which everything will be controlled either by a phone app or remote or a voice program, whichever you like. This project idea will help you maintain the whole house. ‘An AI Programmed Voice Assistant’ - which I call – “ANDY” which can help you to make the light go off and on, can unlock and lock your main door while you are in your room, move the curtains, play any song, any colour of lighting that you need and can do many more things because everything in the house will be programmed.
In this program the centre of taking commands will be “ANDY”, you just have to give a command to ANDY and he will do anything manually or will forward the command to the robots, which can do the physical stuff.
There will be AI robots (that look like humans) which will do everything you need them to, like mowing, sweeping, cleaning, moving the furniture as you wish, making food for family dinner or parties and many more tasks.
ANDY can be a great asset to your home like whenever there is an emergency like fire, flooding or a fight or anything, ANDY will be programmed the way to detect any situation and act accordingly.
Now the most important topic comes that, “Can someone break into your house ?”
Obviously No, and that’s because every member of the house will be recognised and if a stranger comes to the door, ANDY will automatically ask their name and will ask one of the adults to open the door and if the stranger tries to enter the house forcefully ANDY will automatically call the police and will lock every door of the house until further commanded by any member.
So it’s a safe and friendly SMART HOME for everyone. The robots can even play with the people as they wish, like play xbox, playstation, football, cricket, kahoot, uno, chess, and any more outdoor, indoor and board games too.
ANDY also keep track of everyone’s health, like ‘how many weight did a person lose in 10 days’, number of steps in a day or month, heart rate and many more and can even remind you to take your medicines as prescribed by the doctor.
ANDY can also help small children like play with them, teach them whatever the parents say and many more. ANDY will be programmed to speak all the languages for everyone’s ease.
